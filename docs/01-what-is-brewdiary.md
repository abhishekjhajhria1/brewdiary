# 01 — What brewdiary is

*No code in this file. Just the product.*

## The idea in one breath
brewdiary is a **diary for drinks**. Every night you tap today's date and note what you drank. That's
it. The habit is meant to be tiny — a few taps — so you actually do it every day.

A "drink" is **anything**: an espresso, a glass of wine, a beer, a cocktail, a kombucha, a homemade
brew, a can of something, a cup of tea, a mocktail. brewdiary never judges and never counts calories or
units. It's a **journal**, not a health tracker.

## Why anyone would use it
1. **A calm daily ritual.** Most apps shout. brewdiary is quiet and quick — log tonight's drink, done.
2. **A picture of your consistency.** Each day you log, that day's little square on the calendar gets
   brighter. Miss days and they stay dim. Over weeks the calendar becomes a **mosaic** — a picture of
   your streak. People keep streaks going; that's the hook.
3. **Memory.** Months later you can look back: "what was I drinking last winter?" Photos, notes, moods,
   and places are all there if you added them.
4. **Friends, gently.** You can share an entry with friends, see theirs, give a "cheers", leave a
   comment, host a party, or split the bar tab. But the calendar itself stays private and calm — the
   social part is a separate, optional layer.
5. **A bartender in your pocket.** **Ninkasi**, an AI bartender with a playful "goddess of brewing"
   personality, suggests what to pour based on what you've been drinking — and always offers a great
   non-alcoholic option.

## The main screens (what a user sees)
- **Calendar (home).** A month view. Today is highlighted. Tap any day → a little form slides up → you
  log a drink. The squares brighten as you log. There's a "streak" counter and, tucked below, a card to
  ask Ninkasi.
- **Year mosaic.** Zoom out to see the whole year as one grid of squares — the big "picture of your
  year" reward.
- **Together.** The social hub: friends' recent drinks, "cheers" and comments, **circles** (private
  groups), **parties** (host a night, invite people — even non-users — and it turns into a shared
  recap), gentle opt-in **challenges** inside circles, and a **Split** tool to divide a bar bill like
  Splitwise.
- **You.** Your stats, your streak, a wall of your drink photos, your "to-try" wishlist, a searchable
  history ("shelf"), your collected **moods**, and Settings (theme, sign out, export your data, the
  "Help train Ninkasi" switch).
- **Discover.** A surface that (once fully wired to maps data) points you to top-rated bars and shops
  nearby and lets Ninkasi recommend places. This is also the part meant to eventually **make money**
  (promoting venues, and selling anonymous taste trends to brands — only with consent).
- **Ninkasi.** The AI bartender chat.

## How the habit loop is designed
Open app → tap today → type the drink → pick a one-word **mood** (like "cozy" or "celebratory") → tap
Log. The day's square brightens instantly. Two or three taps, every night. Everything else (streaks,
the mosaic, milestones, your mood collection) is calculated automatically from those logs — you never
have to maintain it.

## What makes it feel different (the design)
- **Calm and minimal**, deliberately *not* looking "AI-generated" or cluttered.
- A **"liquid glass"** look: frosted, translucent panels floating over a soft, drink-colored background,
  in **two themes** (light and dark; dark is the default).
- **Type does the talking** — nice fonts, lots of breathing room, one warm amber accent color.
- Warmth comes from the mosaic filling in and small, satisfying interactions — not from decoration.

## The values baked in (important for a drinks app)
- **Age-gated (18+).** First-time users confirm they're of legal drinking age.
- **Never encourages excess.** Ninkasi always offers a non-alcoholic option and, if someone's drinking
  hard, nudges water and care.
- **Privacy first.** Everything you log is **private by default**. Sharing is a separate, deliberate
  choice. Any data used to improve the AI or sell trends is **opt-in and anonymized**.

## Where it is today
The app is **built and working**: you can create an account, log drinks, see the mosaic and streaks,
add friends, share, make circles and parties, split bills, keep a wishlist, and chat with Ninkasi. It's
saved to a real cloud database so it syncs across devices. What's left is mostly **turning things on**
(plugging in the AI provider key, live maps data) and **training our own AI model** — which is exactly
what the [Founder playbook](08-founder-playbook.md) and [Ninkasi AI](05-the-ninkasi-ai-explained.md)
docs walk you through.
